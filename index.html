About Us

Desi Etsy is India’s own niche e-commerce platform for handmade products. We connect rural and urban artisans to buyers across the country, making authentic handmade goods accessible to everyone. From Varanasi’s handloom sarees to Jaipur’s pottery, every product here represents a legacy of skill, culture, and sustainability.

By shopping with us, you don’t just buy a product — you support livelihoods, preserve heritage, and empower communities.
